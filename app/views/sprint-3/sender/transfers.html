Transfers

You can spend spare levy funds by transferring up to 25% of your previous year's funds to another employer, to pay for their apprenticeship training.

You can make a transfer to any employer of your choice.

You are only responsible for paying the cost of their training with a transfer.

The employer receiving the transfer is responsible for everything else related to that apprenticeship.

The receiving employer will need an Apprenticeship Service account to receive the transfer.

You can find an employer to receive a transfer using our transfers matching service.

  1. Create a pledge
  2. Review and approve applications from matching employers
  3. Make a connection on the service and confirm details of the transfer
