Pledge and transfer your levy funds

To help you find another business to transfer your levy funding to, you can:

  1. Set the criteria of how you want to spend your levy
  2. Create a public funding pledge that is searchable online
  3. Review and approve applications
  4. Connect with your chosen business and confirm details of the transfer
  5. Choose how you want to approve the apprenticeship details

Funding rules

To make a transfer you must comply with the apprenticeship funding rules.

This includes not seeking or accept any payments in exchange for sending a transfer of funds.
