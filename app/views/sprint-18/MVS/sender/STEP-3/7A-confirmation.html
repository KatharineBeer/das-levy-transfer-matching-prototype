Approving the apprentice details

You have approved the pledge application. Apprenticeship details will be added by Behavel Construction Ltd to use the funds. This step needs further approval. There are two options you can choose from:

If you choose to review and check the apprenticeship details it means:

  • you will get a prompt to look at the apprenticeship details when they’re added by the employer
  • you will be able to check the standard, the cost and dates.
  • you can ask the employer to amend details You have the option to reject the application.

If you choose to automatically approve the apprentice details, this means:

  • You do not need to see the apprenticeship details unless something changes
  • You will not see the apprentice standard, cost or start date for the application
  • If costs go over it will be automatically rejected
