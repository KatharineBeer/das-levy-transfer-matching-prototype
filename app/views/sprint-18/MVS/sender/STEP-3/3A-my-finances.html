Transfers allowance information

Your transfer allowance for this financial year

This is calculated every year on 6 April and shows in your account after 20 April.

The levy funds you commit to a transfer will be paid from your account monthly over the duration of the apprenticeship. This means you are committing funds over multiple financial years.

Transfer payments will leave your account before payments for your own apprenticeships.

Find out more detailed information (opens in new tab or window)

Pledges and direct transfers

This is an estimate of the amount allocated for your transfers in this financial year. It includes any existing transfer commitments carried over from last year.

It uses an estimate of monthly payments based on the planned start date of training and the 20% completion payment.

It adds the pledges you have made this year to the ongoing transfer commitments you have made in previous years.
