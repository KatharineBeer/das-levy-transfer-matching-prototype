More details about the status

The approval status shows the status of each application.

It could be:

  • awaiting approval
  • approved, awaiting your acceptance
  • unsuccessful
  • funds available to use
  • withdrawn

If your application has been approved, you'll need to accept the funding offer by selecting the opportunity.

Funds will show as available to use if your application has been approved and you have acccepted the offer. You'll then need to add your apprentices and their training provider to your account.

The status shows as withdrawn if you have cancelled your application or decided to decline an offer of funding.
