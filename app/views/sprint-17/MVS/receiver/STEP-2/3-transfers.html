Information about transfers

Your transfer allowance for this financial year

You can use unspent levy funds in your account by transferring up to 25% of your previous year's funds to other organisations.

This is called your transfer allowance.

It is calculated every year on 6 April and shows in your account after 20 April.

The organisations receiving transferred funds can use this to pay for apprenticeship training and assessment.

The levy funds you commit to a transfer will be paid from your account monthly over the duration of the apprenticeship. This means you are committing funds over multiple financial years.

Transfer payments will leave your account before payments for your own apprenticeships.

Allocated for transfers in this financial year

This is an estimate of the amount allocated for your transfers in this financial year and includes any existing transfer commitments carried over from last year.

It uses an estimate of monthly payments based on the planned start date of training and the 20% EPA payment made at the end of training.

It adds the pledges you have made this year to the ongoing transfer commitments you have made in previous years.

Remaining transfer funds

This is an estimate of what remains for you to pledge to transfers in this financial year.
