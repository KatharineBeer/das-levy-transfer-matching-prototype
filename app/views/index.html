Levy transfer matching prototype

What is Levy transfer matching?

Levy transfer matching service allow employeers and trainers to manage apprenticeships funds transfers. This service is part of the Manage Apprenticeship project.

All documents and links are being collected on a Confluence page.

This documents the Discovery and Alpha phase of the {{serviceName}} project, January 2021 onwards.
