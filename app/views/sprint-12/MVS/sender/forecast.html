About the forecast

The forecast uses an estimated total cost of training, based on the funding band maximum for this apprenticeship.

The yearly estimated costs are based on the planned start date of training and the 20% EPA payment made at the end of training. It uses the financial year starting April.

As the estimated costs are based on information provided in the application to receive transfer funding, and details such as planned start and end dates and final agreed costs may change, the forecast can only be an estimate.
