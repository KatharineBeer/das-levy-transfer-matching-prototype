Further information on your transfer allowance calculation

Your transfer allowance in the next financial year will be calculated and shown in your account by the end of April and is based on the total amount of levy you declared during the previous tax year.

We are unable to calculate transfer allowances in advance.

If your workforce/annual pay bill is at a steady level then we would not envisage your allowance fluctuating by much; we cannot guarantee this as it depends on your annual pay bill and any potential growth/downsizing/restructuring.
