About transfers

As a business of any size, you can use this service to apply for funding from large businesses that want to fund 100% of your apprenticeship training costs.

They can fund your apprenticeship training by transferring some of their apprenticeship levy.

As a business looking for this funding, you will need:

  • an apprenticeship service account to apply for and receive funding
  • to think about the type of apprenticeship training you need
  • to think about how many apprentices you need

You can:

  • browse details of funding opportunities
  • apply to be considered for funding
  • apply to more than one opportunity

Getting 95% apprenticeship funding from the government instead

Businesses that don't pay the apprenticeship levy can get 95% of their apprenticeship training costs funded by the government, so they only pay 5%.

So if you're unsuccessful in getting funding from a large business to pay for 100% of your training costs, you'll still only have to pay 5%.

To get 95% government funding you'll need to log into or register for an apprenticeship service account.
