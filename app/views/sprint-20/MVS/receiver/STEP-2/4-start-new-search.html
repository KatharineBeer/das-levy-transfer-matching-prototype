Find a business to fund apprenticeship training

As a business of any size, you can use this service to apply for funding from large businesses that want to fund 100% of your apprenticeship training and assessment costs.

They can fund your apprenticeship training by transferring some of their apprenticeship levy.

As a business looking for this funding, you will need to:

  • know the type of apprenticeship training you need

You can:

  • use a filter to find the most suitable opportunities for your business
  • browse details of funding opportunities
! Warning You'll need an apprenticeship service account to apply to be considered for funding.

Funds from a transfer will be available to use from 27th October.
Read more detailed information about how large businesses transfer their levy funds to other organisations.
